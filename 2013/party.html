March 30-31, 2013

San Diego, CA

Party

This year's party is in a new location: The Pennant in Mission Beach. Look at the map. The shortest way to get there from the fields is literally to swim there. Not that we'd suggest anything like that...

  • You will need wristbands to get in. Captains, don't leave the fields Saturday without wristbands for your team.
  • The party is 21+ (sorry, young'uns) and you can NOT bring outside alcohol. It's a bar.
  • The bar tab opens at 9:00pm and goes until we run out of money. Once you're buying your own drinks, it is cash only.
  • The bar closes at 2am.
  • Street parking outside the bar is scarce. Park just north of the bar in the public lot marked on the map. This lot is open until 2am but you will probably not get ticketed, towed, or blocked in if your car is left there after 2.
  • Remember, friends don't let friends drink and drive (or swim) home.
