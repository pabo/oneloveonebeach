One Love One Beach 2016

San Diego's 15th annual mixed beach ultimate tournament

One Love One Beach is a 24-team, mixed 4v4 beach charity tournament in Mission Beach, San Diego.

The 16th annual OLOB will be April 1 - 2, 2017.

Help us celebrate OLOB's Sweet 16 Party. The hardest part to throwing a party is picking the theme, so each team will bring its own party theme to the fields. No duplicate themes allowed!

As always, OLOB proceeds go to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society. Since 2002, we've donated over $23,000 to this cause. We encourage you to donate and invite your teammates to donate as well, either online or in person at the tournament.

Thanks to team and individual donations last year, DUDE proudly donated another $3,000 to LLS! A special thank you goes to No Pants Party, O'Lobsters, Sandy Again, and Off in the Sand for going so far above and beyond in their donations.

Please join us this year in raising funds for the worthy cause of LLS and in celebrating 16 years of OLOB!
