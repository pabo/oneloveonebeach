One Love One Beach 2015

San Diego's 14th annual mixed beach ultimate tournament

OLOB 2015 was a huge success!

As always, OLOB proceeds go to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society. In 2015 we made our largest donation ever of $3,000. We want to thank the efforts of our TD, Alisha Stoun, as well as the teams whose bid donations went above and beyond our suggested $250 donation.

Thanks again to:

  • Risky Bidness of Phoenix, Arizona
  • O'Lobsters of SoCal
  • Love Yourself of San Diego, CA

Since 2002, we've donated over $20,000 to this cause. We encourage you to donate on your own!

- Your OLOB Crew
