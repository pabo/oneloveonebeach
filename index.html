One Love One Beach 2016

San Diego's 15th annual mixed beach ultimate tournament

One Love One Beach is a 24-team, mixed 4v4 beach charity tournament in Mission Beach, San Diego. We're announcing the dates early this year to give you time to get your teams and bids together.

The 15th annual(!) OLOB will be held April 16-17, 2016.

As always, OLOB proceeds go to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society. Since 2002, we've donated over $20,000 to this cause. We encourage you to donate and invite your teammates to donate as well, either online or in person at the tournament.

Last year, we had our most successful fundraising year yet. We'd like to thank three teams and their captains that went above and beyond our suggested donation to make this possible:

  • Will Gander, Risky Bidness (Phoenix, AZ)
  • Darin Woolpert, O'Lobsters (SoCal)
  • Brett Schellenberg, Love Yourself (San Diego, CA)
