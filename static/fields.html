Fields

There are a few rules to follow at our field site. Please respect these rules so that we can continue to use this wonderful beach for OLOB.

  • No alcohol. In past years, police have stopped by to check, and some players have been fined. Getting caught with alcohol puts our tournament in jeopardy. Please don't do it.
  • No amplified music, except by battery power. We're looking at you, ROCKtopus!
  • No dogs.
  • No glass. For everyone's safety.
  • The southern end of the point is a protected area. Please keep out!
  • Do not park illegally. Mainly, don't block anyone in. There is overflow parking in the paved lot to the northwest.

Directions

From I-5 South:
Take the Sea World Drive exit. Turn right at the stop light which puts you on Sea World Drive. Go past Sea World and take the West Mission Bay Drive onramp which loops around onto West Mission Bay Drive. Continue on West Mission Bay Drive over the bridge to the traffic light intersection and get in left turn lane (Mariner's Way). Turn left and then left again and continue on road until you come to the picnic shelter (blue roof canopy area). The grass area from the comfort station (restroom - brown building) to the sand area is Mariner's Point.

From I-5 North:
Take 8 West/Beaches to Sports Arena/West Mission Bay Drive exit. Turn right at light (this is Ingraham Street) and cross bridge. Continue on Ingraham for about 1 mile and then take the West Mission Bay Drive onramp which loops around onto West Mission Bay Drive. Continue on West Mission Bay Drive over the bridge to the traffic light intersection and get in left turn lane (Mariner's Way). Turn left and then left again and continue on road until you come to the picnic shelter (blue roof canopy area). The grass area from the comfort station (restroom - brown building) to the sand area is Mariner's Point.
