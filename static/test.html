Welcome to One Love One Beach

San Diego's 14th annual mixed beach ultimate tournament

OLOB is back and later than ever!

The tournament will be held April 18-19, 2015 on Mission Bay.

As always, OLOB proceeds go to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society. Since 2002, we've donated over $15,000 to this cause. We encourage you to donate and invite your teammates to donate as well, either online or in person at the tournament.

This year, our tournament coincides with an AUDL game (San Diego Growlers vs. Los Angeles Aviators). All weekend you can play and watch as much ultimate as you can handle! We'll send out the details once they are finalized.

Registration is open now and the 24-team tournament fills up quickly, so send in your bids ASAP. To register:

  1. Go read the field rules. Really. We'll wait right here.
  2. Email oneloveonebeach@gmail.com with the following info:
    • team name, location
    • captain's name, email address, and phone number
    • seeding request (1 is highest, 24 is lowest)
    • team history, including past OLOB's attended and results
    • inspirational quote
  3. Paypal us (suggested donation is $250*) at dudeultimate@gmail.com. If your bid is not accepted, we will refund your full bid fee promptly.
  4. After you submit your bid, keep an eye on the list of accepted bids.

*DUDE and OLOB don't make a dime from this tournament -- this ain't no Lei Out. After covering the tournament costs, we donate 100% of the remaining money to the LLS. $250 is one of the lowest tournament fees around and the more you donate, the more goes to LLS. However, if your team can't spare the suggested donation we will not deny your bid based solely on that. Just send us your sob story and we'll figure something out.

Don't have a team? Need more ladies? Need more dudes? (what?!) Head over to the discussion page to humblebrag. Maybe you'll get picked up.
